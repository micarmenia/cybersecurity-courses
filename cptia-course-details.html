About CREST

CREST (Council of Registered Ethical Security Testers) is an international not-for-profit, membership organization representing the technical information security industry. CREST provides internationally recognized accreditation for organizations and individuals providing penetration testing, cyber incident response, and threat intelligence services.

CREST's Mission

CREST works to set and maintain professional standards, provide accreditation to companies and individuals who demonstrate competence and capability in specified areas, and share knowledge and best practices across the information security sector.

Certification & Exam

Upon successful completion of this training program and passing the CPTIA examination, participants will receive the internationally recognized CREST Practitioner Threat Intelligence Analyst certification.

Exam Details

  • CPTIA exam voucher (Pearson VUE) included in course fee
  • Multiple-choice format testing knowledge and application
  • Administered at authorized Pearson VUE testing centers
  • Valid globally and recognized by employers worldwide
  • Demonstrates competency in threat intelligence analysis to CREST standards

Career Impact

The CPTIA certification is highly valued by employers in both public and private sectors, particularly in regulated industries such as finance, healthcare, and critical infrastructure. It demonstrates your capability to work as a professional threat intelligence analyst according to internationally accepted standards.
